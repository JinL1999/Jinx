INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME

Our internship programme is designed to provide highly motivated undergraduates from various field of study with the opportunity to gain invaluable real-world working experience and skills across our diverse businesses. Discover the opportunities that await you at WCT!


REQUIREMENTS


  • Qualified candidate who is pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in the relevant fields
  • Possess minimum CGPA 3.0 or equivalent
  • Proactive, enthusiastic and resourceful
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

How to apply?


Please type your resume with a short write-up of less than 200 words on your goals as well as what you aim to accomplish through the internship at WCT. Internships are subject to placement availability.
